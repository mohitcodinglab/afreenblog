AFREEN'S BLOG



Hello, welcome to my blog!



My name is Afreen Ali, and I currently attend the University of Waterloo for Global Business and Digital Arts. I love being a student along with being an aspiring designer, and this blog is a representation of that! I am originally from Toronto but now reside in Waterloo for school.



The purpose of this blog is to showcase my interests, which are going to concerts and traveling to different places within Toronto along with the rest of the world. I hope this blog inspires people to go to concerts and hit up some of my favorite spots in Toronto!



Aside from my passion for design, I love going to the gym and try my absolute best to go at least four times a week. I enjoy lifting weights and hope to one day be a personal trainer. I have an elder sister who lives with me here in Waterloo since we both attend the same university. In our apartment, we have a pet dog named KeKe. KeKe is two years old, and I absolutely adore her!
