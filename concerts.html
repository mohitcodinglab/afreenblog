I love going to concerts in the city and I think it is genuinely one of my favorite hobbies at the moment. I’ve been to concerts with my friend, sister, and even alone! Out of all the experiences, I have to say going to concerts alone is my favorite! I enjoy it the most partly because I used to be so scared of doing things alone and now I appreciate it so much.

Olivia Rodrigo


Date: May 1st 2022
Overall Rating: 8/10
Venue: Massey Hall

Experience:


  • It was my first concert ever so I was super excited and I went with my best friend at the time.
  • It was super hard to get the tickets so I was extremely lucky to be able to go.
  • I knew every song by heart.
  • The artist came out a bit late but other than that I loved it.

The Weeknd


Date: September, 2023
Overall Rating: 10/10
Venue: Rogers Center

Experience:


  • I got tickets to these concert for my sister as her birthday gift.
  • We got our tickets upgraded for free by a worker so we went from section 500 to section 100!
  • The Weeknd sounded so good in person.
  • I loved the way the stage was set up and the choreography of the dancers.

Nav


Date: April, 2023
Overall Rating: 7/10
Venue: Scotiabank Arena

Experience:


  • I went to this concert with my sister.
  • My sister and I missed the train to go to the concert, which ended up with us missing the first 30 minutes of it.
  • Nav brought out Travis Scott which was amazing.
  • Our seats weren’t the best since we got them really last minute.

Bryson Tiller


Date: September, 2023
Overall Rating: 8.5/10
Venue: History

Experience:


  • This was my first solo concert so I was super nervous and I also had never been to this venue before.
  • It was so fun and I enjoyed it way more than I thought I would.
  • My tickets were for the floor so I got to be super close up to the artist.
  • I made many new friends!

Drake


Date: October, 2023
Overall Rating: 100/10
Venue: Scotiabank Arena

Experience:


  • Drake is my favourite artist so going to his concert in his city was a dream come true.
  • I bought tickets the day before and even though they were super expensive, they were worth it.
  • I knew every single song that was played and loved the vibes.
  • I went alone and am so happy I did because I enjoyed my own company.
