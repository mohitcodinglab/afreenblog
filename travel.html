Toronto

Since I live in Toronto, I have the luxury of exploring unique places and creating wonderful memories. In the summer, I love visiting the AGO (Art Gallery of Toronto) since they always have cool new exhibitions to visit. Asides from museums, I love trying new restaurants in Toronto. My two favouirte ones I tried this year were Bar Poet and Mado.

Montreal

This summer, I went to Montreal with my parents and my sister. While we were there, we went to Old Port and rode on the Ferris Wheel, climbed Mount Royal, and went to this cool rubber duck store. I loved this trip since it was the first time in a while that I got to spend one on one time with my family.
